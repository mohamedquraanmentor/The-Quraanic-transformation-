### 1️⃣ **"About Mentor"** (instead of "Privacy Policy")
- High-ticket clients want to know WHO they're learning from
- Builds trust and authority
- Links to the #about section on the page

### 2️⃣ **"FAQ"** (instead of "Terms")
- Answers common objections
- Reduces friction before application
- Links to the #faq section

### 3️⃣ **"Contact via Email"** (instead of "Contact")
- More professional and specific
- Creates email link: `mentor@quraanictransformation.com`
- Sets expectation of communication method

### 4️⃣ **"Elite Qur'anic Mentorship · By Assessment Only"**
- Reinforces exclusivity
- Reminds visitors this is NOT for everyone
- "By Assessment Only" = premium positioning
- Creates desire through scarcity
